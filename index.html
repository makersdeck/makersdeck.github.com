The Maker's Deck

What is this?

The Maker's Deck is a set of collectible trading cards.

I love trading cards, but am neither interested in sports or fantasy games. So made the cards I would want to buy.

Can I get some?

They will soon available Etsy. I am also preparing a Kickstarter-campaign. Get notice when whose things happen:

Will this be open source?

Yes. Besides releasing all files and artwork on Github , I will also share the process of how to make cards yourself.

The plan is also to build a webapp to generate cards, make printable sheets and find other people in real life to trade cards with.
